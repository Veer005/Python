Asphalt Services

Asphalt is at the center of what we do. From new installations to overlays, resurfacing, and sealcoating, our goal is to deliver smooth, durable pavement that drains correctly and looks sharp for years.

Commercial asphalt

Asphalt parking lot installation & repair

A well-built parking lot gives customers a good first impression and keeps traffic moving safely. We install new lots and repair existing ones for office buildings, churches, retail centers, and HOAs.

  • Site evaluation, measurements, and base preparation.
  • Proper grading for drainage and safe vehicle movement.
  • Installation of hot-mix asphalt and machine compaction.
  • Line striping, accessible parking, and traffic markings.

Residential asphalt

Asphalt driveway installation & repair

Driveways see daily use and weather. When they crack, sink, or break apart, we can repair or replace them with a new asphalt surface built on a solid base.

  • Removal of failing or severely cracked asphalt when needed.
  • Base repair, grading, and compaction for a solid foundation.
  • New driveway layouts and extensions for added parking space.
  • Attention to water flow so runoff goes away from your home and garage.

We provide clear instructions on how long to stay off the driveway after paving, and how to care for it in the first year.

Cost-effective option

Asphalt overlay

If your pavement is worn but the base is still in good condition, an overlay can be a smart choice. We apply a new layer of asphalt over the existing surface and address problem areas first.

  • Suitable when cracking and wear are moderate, not severe.
  • Can improve ride quality and appearance without full replacement.
  • Often paired with patching where underlying damage exists.
Protection

Asphalt sealcoating

Sealcoating helps protect asphalt from sun, salt, oil, and water intrusion. While it doesn’t fix structural problems, it can extend the life of driveways and parking lots when done at the right time.

  • Recommended after repairs or a new installation has properly cured.
  • Helps resist surface wear and keeps pavement looking uniform.
  • Best when combined with crack filling and patching as needed.
